Today I want to share some of my favorite Fallout 76 mods. I’m not a mod author myself, but I’m always on the 
lookout for mods that make the game smoother, clearer, and generally more enjoyable. Over time 
I’ve collected a small set that I keep coming back to.

# List

Here’s a list of the mods I currently use and recommend:
- [Lockpick Bar 76](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/61)
- [Recipes and Plans Glow](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/84)
- [Perk Loadout Manager](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/124)
- [plant_glow](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/199?tab=description)
- [ore_glow](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/207)
- [No Silo Doors](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/1198)
- [Better Targeting HUD](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/2081)
- [Invent O Matic Stash](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/2335)
- [Invent O Matic Pipboy](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/2324)
- [Smaller Explosive Effects](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/2408)
- [Improved Workbench](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/2576)
- [BuffsMeter](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/2821)
- [V.A.T.S. Priority](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout76/mods/3297)

I also use a few private mods that I can’t share publicly. If you’re interested in those or want more details 
about my setup, feel free to contact me.

# What’s next?

I plan to create videos where I go through these mods one by one, explain why I use them, and show how to install and 
configure them to make your Fallout 76 experience a bit easier.
